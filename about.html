Who are we?

Argyle Hotels Group (Australia) has served our guests with the very finest of Australian hospitality. Argyle Hotels Group is ranked Top 10 for Foreign Hotel Groups, Top 50 Global Hotel Groups and Top 60 Hotel Groups in Asia. AHG comprises of 13 distinct brands ranging from mid-scale to luxury and long-term stays; Argyle Grand Hotel, Argyle Boutique, Argyle Resorts, Argyle Hotel, Rejoice by Argyle, Argyle Suites, Argyle Waverton Apartments, Ausotel, Ausotel Smart, Ausotel Suites, Ausotel WOW and Metro. From luxury hotels to boutiques, from resorts to serviced apartments, explore the world with AHG and embrace the new opportunities life has to offer.

Smart rooms.

Our rooms redefine luxury with smart technology seamlessly integrated into your experience. Motion sensors delicately respond to your presence, illuminating the room with a gentle glow as you enter. Navigate your surroundings effortlessly with a bed control panel that gracefully operates electric curtains, allowing you to customize your ambiance at the touch of a button.
